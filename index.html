Adam Binstock- Ergonomics Health Association

1.

Create a dedicated workspace in your home

2.

Make sure you have a comfortable and distraction-free ergonomic environment

3.

Creating a regular work routine and stick to it religiously

4.

Create work/life boundaries in order to switch off

5.

Stay connected and engaged with colleagues
